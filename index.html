Executive Summary

CHEF Catfish Co. is a startup seafood enterprise founded by Ms. Udeh Phebe Chinyere and it is focused on producing and supplying premium-quality catfish and other seafood products to meet the growing demand in Nigeria’s expanding food industry. Our mission is to deliver fresh, nutritious seafood directly to consumers, retailers, and restaurants, ensuring quality, consistency, and reliability. By adopting lean management principles, integrating financial analysis into decision-making, and leveraging advanced project management software, we aim to build a profitable and sustainable business that sets a new standard in Nigeria’s seafood market.

Objectives

  • Operational Excellence: Implement lean management practices to reduce waste, optimize production, and maximize efficiency across all farming and supply operations.
  • Financial Sustainability: Conduct regular financial analyses to assess profitability, guide strategic investments, and maintain healthy cash flow.
  • Resource Optimization: Utilize project management software to streamline resource allocation, track production cycles, and manage inventory in real time.
  • Market Penetration: Secure a competitive market share in Nigeria’s growing seafood industry through strong branding, customer engagement, and targeted distribution channels.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Provide fresh, high-quality seafood products that consistently meet or exceed customer expectations, fostering long-term loyalty and repeat business.

Core Product

CHEF Catfish Co. specializes in the farming and supply of Catfish (Clarias gariepinus), known for its adaptability, rapid growth rate, and high market demand in Nigeria. The company’s production process prioritizes:

  • Freshness – delivering live fish to customers whenever possible.
  • Quality – maintaining strict hygiene and farming standards.
  • Nutritional value – offering a protein-rich food source without the need for chemical preservatives.

Production Layout

StageDescription
FingerlingsHatchlings placed in the first pond; nurtured and fed; water quality monitored.
JuvenileTransferred to second pond after ~2 months; biomass prepared; feeding continues.
Table SizeMoved to third pond; focus on growth and health; schedules tracked via software.
Harvest SizeTransferred to fourth pond for final fattening; harvested for delivery or processing.
Water ManagementFreshwater supply maintained; regular testing of pH, temperature, and oxygen.
Bio-AeratorOxygen supply maintained using bio-aerators.

Lean Management Approach

  • Waste Reduction: Avoid overfeeding, overstocking, and unnecessary energy use.
  • Continuous Improvement: Use feedback loops to refine processes.
  • Efficiency Tracking: Monitor KPIs to identify and address inefficiencies.
  • Quicker production cycles and faster returns on investment.

Use of Project Management Software

  • Schedule feeding, maintenance, and harvesting tasks.
  • Track resource allocation (feed, water, labor).
  • Maintain digital logs for production cycles, expenses, and sales performance.
  • Generate performance reports for management and investors.
